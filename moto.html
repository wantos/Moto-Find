About us


Looking for a convenient, comfortable way to get around? You have come to the right place; at MotoHire, we pride ourselves in providing our clients with the best transport services.


Regardless of your needs, we offer our clients a range of services; from bridal cars, to self-drive, airport drop-offs and pick-ups or just a driver to take you wherever you want. We cater to your needs, whether fancy or practical.


Established in 2018, MotoHire is the brainchild of Emmanuel Wantono, Roland Bisaaso, Jaelle Amongi, and Vallerie Karungi. We are a team that is always readily available to help you, regardless of office hours or non-business days.


So, Why motoHire?


Well, we are professional and always aim at elevating our customer experience.


Our cars are in tip-top condition and are well maintained. We have a large variety of cars that will suit your every need.

FAQ'S


  1. Qn :How can a client get a car in case they are upcountry?
    Ans:Worry not, we have drivers readily available to deliver the car of your choice to anywhere you are.


  2. Qn :What are the payment terms?
    Ans :Clients are required to pay a 50% deposit on recieving the car and the remainder of the payment is made when returning the car.


  3. Qn :What happens if I have an accident?
    Ans :Our cars are well maintained and are covered under comprehensive insurance. However, apart from the usual wear and tear of our vehicles, any damage on the car that is clearly the fault of the client will be paid for by the client and not the company.


  4. Qn :What requirements do i need to rent a car with MotoHire?
    Ans :All we need is a valid driving permit and a 50% deposit.


  5. Qn :Do you offer chaffeur services?
    Ans :We offer our clients the option of both self drive or chaffeur services depending on your needs and your budget.
